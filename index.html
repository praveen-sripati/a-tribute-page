Mahatma Gandhi

Bapu who maintained peace with the help of peace.

Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi's life

  • 1869 - Birth at Porbandar.
  • 1882 - Marriage with Kasturbai, aged 13.
  • 1888 - Son, Harilal born.Reached Southampton, England.Enrolled at the Inner Temple, Inn of Court.
  • 1891 - Called to the Bar.
  • 1892 - Son, Manilal born.
  • 1893 - Refused to remove his Paghadi; left the court.Thrown off the first class carriage of the train at Pietermartizburg Station.Read Tolstoy's 'Kingdom of God is Within You'.
  • 1894 - Founded the 'Natal Indian Congress'.
  • 1897 - Son, Ramdas born.
  • 1900 - Assisted Kasturbai in the delivary of son Devadas.
  • 1904 - Established 'Phoenix Settlement'.
  • 1906 - Advent of Satyagraha at Empire Theatre, Johannesburg, where people took a pledge with God as their witness, to oppose the Black Act.
  • 1908 - The term 'Satyagraha' adopted based on Maganlal Gandhi's fomulation 'Sadagraha'.Awarded two months' rigourous imprisonment for entry into the Transvaal without permit.
  • 1909 - Left for England as a member of the Indian Delegation.Wrote 'Hind Swarajya' aboard the ship 'Kildonan Castle'.'Hind Swarajya' published in 'Indian Opinion'.
  • 1913 - MKG released from jail unconditionally with others, before the completion of the period of imprisonement.
  • 1915 - Visited Santiniketan.
  • 1915 - Visited Kumbh Mela, Hardwar.
  • 1920 - Returned the honours to the Government, commencement of Non-co-operation.
  • 1922 - Gandhiji and Shankerlal Banker arrested.Charged of sedition for the authorship of three articles in Young India.Trial held at Ahmedabad Circuit House; Judge Broomfield awarded Gandhiji a sentence of six years' imprisonment.
  • 1924 - The publication of 'Dakshin Africana Satyagrahano Itihas' in Navajivan.
  • 1925 - The publication of 'Autobiography or The Story of My Experiments with Truth' commences in Young India.
  • 1930 - Wrote to the Viceroy about his intention to break the Salt Law.Dandi March commences from Satyagraha Ashram.Broke the Salt Law.Arrested and sent to Yeravda Prison.
  • 1931 - Attended (in his loin-cloth) the Royal reception for the delegates of the Second Round Table Conference at the Buckingham Palace at the invitation of Emperor George V.
  • 1932 - Arrested and sent to Yeravda for indefinite period; Vallabhbhai fellow prisoner.Letter to Ramsay Macdonald declaring his intentions to fast unto death against the Communal Award.
  • 1936 - Moved to Segaon from Wardha.Presided over the 12th Gujarati Sahitya Parishad.Travancore temples opened to Harijans.
  • 1939 - Sir Maurice Gwyer declared award in favour of the people of Rajkot; Muslims and Bhayats demonstrate against Gandhiji and disrupt a prayer meeting.
  • 1942 - Gandhiji and Congress Working Committee members arrested; Gandhiji imprisoned at the Aga Khan Palace.'Quit India' resolution adopted; gave the call 'Do or Die'.
  • 1945 - Congress Working Committee members released from Ahmedanagar Fort Prison. Ban on Congress lifted.
  • 1946 - Handed over the responsibility of Harijan Journals to Kaka Kalelkar, Kishorelal Mashruwala and Narahari Parikh.
  • 1947 - Indefinite fast against the communal violence in Calcutta.
  • 1948 - Took three bullets on his chest. Godse the assassin.
  • 1948 - Cremated on the banks of the Yamuna by Ramdas.
